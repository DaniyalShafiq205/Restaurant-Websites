Maaemo Giftcard

If you would like to give someone of the gift of experiencing Maaemo then you can order gift cards from us. You can select from the options below or choose your amount by adding quantity of 500 kr.

Gift cards can either be sent by post or electronically to your email (please allow 5-7 days for paper gift cards to arrive, and 24-48 hours for electronic gift cards). Please select the delivery method you require when ordering. It's also possible to add a comment or greeting to be printed on the gift card in the next window.

Please note that it may be hard to get a table on your desired date, and even with a gift card you still have to make your reservation through our online booking process.
